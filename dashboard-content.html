Square Footage Analysis

Resolving the discrepancy between public records (2,199) and Zillow (2,516)

Key FindingThe Cleveland County public record shows 2,199 sq ft of finished living area. Zillow and Realtor.com both report 2,516 sq ft. The difference is 317 sq ft, which aligns almost exactly with the enclosed rear living / sunroom conversion (estimated ~25' × 13' = ~325 sq ft).

Verification Notes

The original FLOOR PLAN.pdf is a rasterized scan of a hand-drawn architectural sheet. OCR was unreliable for exact room labels and dimensions, so the strongest practical conclusion remains that the public-record gap is explained by the rear conversion space.

Action ItemVerify whether the enclosed rear space was permitted and whether the county record should be updated. If it was never permitted, an appraiser may still default back to 2,199 sq ft for financed offers.
